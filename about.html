What is this?

This is a vulnerability scanner that will check your website for vulnerabilites that hackers can use to break in and steal unauthorized data. Other vulnerability scanners can be pretty complex and complicated to understand to develops who don't have knowledge in cybersecurity. Our scanner will limit that complexity and complicateness as our scanner is straightfoward and easy to use and understand.
